Experience

Island hopping made easy

A well-priced day cruise from Athens, designed to pack beach time, culture and scenery into one effortless day.

Depart from the bustling city and head out across the Saronic Gulf. If you are looking for beach, sun, culture and striking island landscapes in a single experience, this cruise is built for you.

Feel the salty breeze, watch the coastline fade behind you and settle into life on board. Swim in clear blue water, wander through island alleyways and come back to Athens with a full day of memories.

Food on board is prepared with quality ingredients and Greek olive oil, sourced from trusted farms and producers around the country.

Destinations

Three Saronic Gulf gems

Discover a different side of Athens as you hop between island alleys, fishing harbours and turquoise bays.

First stop
Culture & pistachios

Aegina

Wander the harbourfront, taste Aegina pistachios, visit local shops or enjoy a coffee by the sea. On most days you will have time to explore at your own pace.

Neoclassical facades, island life and a relaxed atmosphere just off Athens.

Second stop
Bike & beach options

Agistri

A green island with crystal-clear waters. Stay on board to relax or join an optional eco-friendly bike tour to explore pine-covered landscapes and hidden coves.

Laid-back, scenic and easy-going – perfect for a swim and a stroll.

Swim stop
Depending on weather

Aponisos • Moni • Metopi

Your swimming bay is chosen according to sea and wind conditions on the day. Expect inviting turquoise water and plenty of time to swim straight from the boat.

Jump into the sea, float, snorkel and soak in the blue.

FAQ & about

Practical information

What should I bring with me?
Bring your swimwear, towel, sunscreen, hat and a light cover for the breeze. A camera and a small day bag are also recommended.
Is the cruise suitable for children?
Families are welcome. Children should always be supervised by an adult, especially during swimming stops and while moving around the boat.
What happens in case of bad weather?
The route and swimming spot may change or the cruise may be rescheduled according to safety guidelines and port authority instructions.
Can I book the boat privately?
Yes. BestCruise offers private charters for groups, events and incentives. Contact us with your preferred date and group size.
